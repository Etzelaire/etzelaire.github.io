jordivargashere - A Travel Vlog

I have always craved for travelling the globe but never had the means to do it. After years of failed attempts of achieving a lifestyle that enabled me to pursue my dream I think I'm getting quite close to it. Hence, the inception of this project - my adventure around the neighborhood. Seeking local experiences anywhere I go and bringing to you guys my two pences on each of my destinations. Hope you like it!
