EXPAT LIVING IN SAUDI

As of summer 2022 I embarked into what I deem the most interesting journey of my life. The stars aligned to perfection for me to pursue the Saudi dream. Being playing tennis my whole life I decided to make a career out of it, so I became a tennis coach back in my country. This area of expertise isn't easily rewarded economically and that's why I had always had the thought in the back of my mind of moving abroad and offer my services for a fair value.

Saudi was the wake up call I needed. I had always thought but never taken action, this was my time, this was a signal and I would not turn my back to it. There's something romantic about leaving everything behind, getting your bags to the airport and bidding farewell to your life. We all get so caught up into the cozy comfort zone that forget about the thrill of exploration, the anticipation of the unknown and the riches these experiences will bring us.

I like cultures, I like people and I am always thirsty for knowledge. I am a strong believer on the value any interaction with anybody can bring to one. If I could I would experience with as many people as possible as many different situations.

WHY SAUDI?

I've always been curious about the arabic culture and language. Moreover, these last years arabic countries have risen in power due to their crude exports, which has propelled their infrastructure development making them a very interesting destination.

Everything about the kingdom is vastly different from what we're used to back in Europe. That's one of the main reasons why I decided to embark on this journey.

It is also an opportunity to explore further lands. Being established in the far East of Africa offers a great opportunity to visit the sorrounding countries a piece of cake.
